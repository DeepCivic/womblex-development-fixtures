                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Glossary                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Definition
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Number of MSO entities                                                                                                                                                                             Total number of entities with a stockholding obligation in each year
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   Stocks required under MSO                                                                       Volume required to be held on each obligation day, sum of all MSO entities (ML). Excludes any temporary reductions approved under section 18 of the Fuel Security Act 2021
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Stocks held under MSO                                                                                                                                   Volume reported on each obligation day, sum of all MSO entities, quarterly average of each obligation day (ML)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Minimum stocks held under MSO                                                                                                                                                                             Minimum volume reported in the quarter, sum of all MSO entities (ML)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Maximum stocks held under MSO                                                                                                                                                                             Maximum volume reported in the quarter, sum of all MSO entities (ML)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Average stock held under MSO (Days equivalent) [1] Volume of stock held on each obligation day, sum of all MSO entities, converted to equivalent days of consumption. Consumption is average daily consumption for previous 12 months. Quarterly average of days equivalent for each obligation day
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Instances of reports below required volume [2]                                                                                                                                                                Number of instances an entity reported volume below their stockholding obligation
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          ML                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Megalitres; Million litres
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Notes                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
[1] In December quarter 2024 diesel stocks held under the MSO were equivalent to 31 days. Target days for 2024-25 are 32 days for importers and 20 days for refiners. For public reporting purposes, stocks held and days equivalent for refiners and importers are combined due to commercial sensitivity.  Refiners have lower target days than importers which means their combined average can be below the target days for importers.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                     [2] There have been 2690 MSO reports by entities since the MSO introduction on 1 July 2023. Of this, there were 3 instances where an entity reported stock volume below their obligation. The total stock held by all entities exceeded the total obligation for all reporting periods during the year.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Minimum stockholding obligation for fuel in Australia 2024-25 statistics.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Major fuel importers and refiners must hold minimum volumes of diesel, gasoline and kerosene.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Column chart comparing minimum required and average held for diesel, gasoline and kerosene.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Column one: Diesel minimum required 2485 ML, average held 2968 ML.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Column two: Gasoline minimum required 1070 ML, average held 1743 ML.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Column three: Kerosene minimum required 581 ML, average held 799 ML.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      All entities held higher average stocks of diesel, gasoline and kerosene than required in 2024-25.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Oil icon showing diesel 19% above, gasoline 63% above and kerosene 38% above required volume.
Stocks can be held in Australia and on water in Australia's exclusive economic zone. Stocks of gasoline, kerosene and diesel include eligible crude oil and unfinished products held at refineries. These inclusions means MSO statistics are not comparable with gasoline, kerosene and diesel stocks reported in the Australian Petroelum Statistics.
